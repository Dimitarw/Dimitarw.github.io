Sightseeing – Times Square

Times Square is one of New York City's most famous landmarks, known for its bright billboards, crowds, and lively atmosphere day and night.

Walking in Central Park

Central Park offers peaceful walking paths, lakes, and green spaces—an ideal escape from the busy city streets.

Biking on Brooklyn Bridge

Cycling across the Brooklyn Bridge gives you stunning views of Manhattan and the East River, making it one of NYC’s most iconic outdoor activities.

Food – NYC Street Food

New York is famous for its street food, from hot dogs and pretzels to food trucks serving flavors from all over the world.

Museum Visit – The Met

The Metropolitan Museum of Art is one of the largest art museums globally, featuring masterpieces from ancient history to modern times.

Boat/Ferry Activity – Staten Island Ferry

The Staten Island Ferry offers a free ride with fantastic views of the Statue of Liberty and the Manhattan skyline.
